Lady tasting tea - Wikipedia

In the design of experiments in statistics, the lady tasting tea is a randomized experiment devised by Ronald Fisher and reported in his book The Design of Experiments (1935). The experiment is the original exposition of Fisher's notion of a null hypothesis, which is never proved or established, but is possibly disproved, in the course of experimentation. ㅤ
